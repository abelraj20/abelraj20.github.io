About Me

My name is Abel Rajesh. I was born on the 21st of March 2004 in Kerala, India. At the age of 2, I moved to Ireland after my mother secured a job opportunity here. Growing up in Ireland has been an enriching experience, allowing me to blend the cultural heritage of my birthplace with the dynamic environment of my new home.

This academic path has fueled my passion for coding and harnessing data to draw meaningful insights.
